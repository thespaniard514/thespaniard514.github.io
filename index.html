About Me

What am I all about?

Programming

I studied computer science at Fort Lewis College. After graduation I continued my education in the field working with MSSQL, Python/Django, Ruby on Rails, AngularJS, VueJS, Java, MySql, and anything else I could get my hands on. I'm always ready to jump in and help out with whatever language or problem there is. I love building and problem solving.

Hobbies

Crafting, making, hacking, tinkering. Whatever you call it, I like it. In the same vein of programming, it is fun to come up with creative projects to build and utilize in every day life. I have been a homebrewer for many years. Beer is an amazing beverage. I'm a member of our local Maker Lab where I have access to laser cutters, 3d printers, CNC routers, welding, sewing, vinyl, etc. It has been a great way to keep my curiosity in different industries fed.

Durango Life

Durango, Colorado is an amazing place to live. The nearby mountains offer many options for outdoor sports throughout the year. I enjoy skiing at Purgatory Resort, mountain biking, hiking, disc golfing, and the Animas River calls my name when it is too hot.
